How did we approach the problem?

Besides gathering information about Kings Of Leon across Sziget Festival key markets we focused on creating a competitive analysis against two similar sized bands booked in the past by Sziget Festival (Foo Fighters and The Killers).

We looked into data related to: fan base size and fan engagement across key markets, upcoming and past shows and album releases.
